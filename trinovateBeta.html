Trinovate Synergy 1.0

Details

Trinovate Synergy 1.0 is a premier hackathon and innovation fest organized by Trinity Academy of Engineering (TAE), Pune. This event is designed to bring together brilliant minds from diverse disciplines, including Engineering, Medical, Arts, Commerce, Agriculture, and MCA, to collaborate and create cutting-edge solutions for real-world challenges.

Trinovate Synergy 1.0 is not just a competition; it is an opportunity for students to innovate, connect, and grow. Participants will gain exposure to industry experts, mentors, and startup networks, fostering a culture of technological advancement and entrepreneurship.
